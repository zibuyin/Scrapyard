This is an APP that will play Danny Go videos

Who is Danny Go?

Danny Go! is a children’s YouTube channel that combines music, movement, and educational content to engage young viewers. Created in 2019 by three childhood friends from Charlotte, North Carolina, the channel has experienced significant growth, boasting over 2.8 million subscribers and more than 2.8 billion views as of early 2025.

Danny Go's Videos

Danny Go!‘s videos are so cringe that they make you laugh for no reason.

Project Goal

The goal of this project is to create an application that plays Danny Go videos and entertains users with humorous and engaging content.
